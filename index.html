INFORMATION

Body Calculator is a tool that helps you to know your daily calorie intake, macronutrients and the amount of calories you need to consume to reach your goals. It is important to know that the results are approximate and may vary depending on the person's metabolism.

Using Body Calculator, you can also track your progress over time and adjust your diet and exercise routine accordingly. Remember to consult with a healthcare professional before making any significant changes to your diet or exercise regimen.

The tool takes into account various factors such as age, gender, weight, height, and activity level to provide personalized recommendations. By understanding your body's needs, you can make more informed decisions about your health and fitness journey.
